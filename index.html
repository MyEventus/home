Welcome to MyEventus
Last Update: 2021Feb19

This site is not yet open to the public. See Getting Started page.

Aim: A SIMPLE way to replace the current multiple Emails, SMS and phone calls when organise events such as Mountain Bike Rides, Drinks, Water sports, Parties, gathering etc for a small group of friends.

You must sign up first, then be manually authorised before your acount will be activated on this site.


We don't all use FBook or other social networking apps!


If we don't know you (friends of friends), you probably will get rejected.

Having said that, if there is enough demand and financial viable then I may be open to public in future.


This sites original intention was to be absolutley free of charge, cost and currently is,

But as user limits and threshold raise (number members join, number of entries in database, number of emails sent.

It will start encuring costs, at which point will become a financial loss, hence only open to limited group at present.

For this reason (and time constraints) this site as linited functionality.


Features
  • Calendar (Read Only at this stage).
  • List of all events (categorised by type).
  • Add members to one or more groups / teams.
  • Notes for directions and meeting place descriptions.
  • Auto email to members apon new, updates and cancellation of events (relagtive to team members).
  • Auto send SMS (Coming soon if users are willing to pay)?
  • Maps (Coming soon)?
  • The sky's the limit given funding and time.

Privacy

The only real personal information of members stored in this system is email addresses and first name (optional).

Hence this site is more for the privacy advocate. We don't collect user info or sell it to 3rd parties.

Your informations is yours!
